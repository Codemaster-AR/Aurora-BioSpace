Introduction

Aurora Biospace respects user privacy and is committed to protecting information handled through the application.

This Privacy Policy explains how Aurora Biospace may collect, use, and process information when users access or use the Aurora Biospace platform.

By using Aurora Biospace, users acknowledge and agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2

About Aurora Biospace

Aurora Biospace is a scientific research and analysis platform that provides access to publicly available space biology and related research resources.

The platform may provide:

  • Access to publicly available NASA-related and GeneLab-related scientific resources
  • Retrieval and exploration of publicly accessible OSDR (Open Science Data Repository) and GeneLab data
  • AI-assisted analysis and informational outputs using Google Gemini AI
  • Search grounding and dynamic information retrieval technologies
Note: Aurora Biospace is an independent application and is not represented as an official NASA service or platform.

Information Collected

Google Authentication Information

Aurora Biospace currently uses Google authentication for user sign-in. When users sign in using Google, Aurora Biospace may receive limited account information made available through Google's authentication systems, which may include:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Basic profile information

Aurora Biospace currently uses this information primarily for authentication and account access purposes.

Usage and Technical Information

Aurora Biospace may automatically collect limited technical and usage-related information necessary for platform operation, security, and performance. This may include:

  • Device and browser configurations
  • Log files and diagnostic telemetry
  • General usage activity records within the application
  • Authentication-related session information

Research and Search Activity

Aurora Biospace may process user searches, viewed resources, and analysis requests in order to provide platform functionality.

Users should avoid submitting sensitive, confidential, or regulated personal information through the platform.
